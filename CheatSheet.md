# Important Commands Cheatsheet

## Tab bar

- (`n`, `<A-,>`): go to prev tab
- (`n`, `<A-.>`): go to next tab
- (`n`, `<A-1>`): go to tab 1
- (`n`, `<A-2>`): go to tab 2
- (`n`, `<A-3>`): go to tab 3
- (`n`, `<A-4>`): go to tab 4
- (`n`, `<A-5>`): go to tab 5
- (`n`, `<A-6>`): go to tab 6
- (`n`, `<A-7>`): go to tab 7
- (`n`, `<A-8>`): go to tab 8
- (`n`, `<A-9>`): go to tab 9
- (`n`, `<A-0>`): go to tab 10
- (`n`, `<A-c>`): close current tab
- (`n`, `<C-p>`): enter pick mode
